About

✦ Founded 2007 · Homestead, Florida

Merge with the
Spirit Within

Rev. Sharon Shaw-MhD is a Doctor of Metaphysical Healing, Ordained Non-Denominational Interfaith Minister, Certified Shadow Work Practitioner, Meditation Instructor, Medical Intuitive, and Soul Clarity Card Therapy Specialist — with nearly two decades of service to clients across South Florida and beyond.

Founded in 2007, Merging Spirit Inc.™ offers a uniquely powerful framework that bridges the 3D world of personality and ego with the 5D soul's karmic mission. Using the Tri-Engine Diagnostic System — combining Cardology, Draconic Astrology, and Attachment Style — Rev. Sharon delivers precision guidance that cuts through surface patterns to reach the root of what is truly happening in a client's life.

As the former host of Inner Lately — a live call-in spiritual radio program with a five-year run on the Women for Women Network with national iHeart Radio distribution — Rev. Sharon has served thousands of listeners and clients who were seeking real answers, honest guidance, and lasting transformation.
